    carlo ancelotti has changed the real madrid plan - Europ

    Carlo Ancelotti has decided to change his game plan for Real Madrid and now play with four players in midfield.According to Spanish newspaper Marca، the Italian coach will play in a 4-4-2 formation for the upcoming matches، with Tchouameni، Camavinga، Valverde and Bellingham playing in midfield. According to Carlo Ancelotti's new plan, one of Rodrygo, Mbappe and Vinicius should be on the reserve bench and be the victim of the plan.

    According to the report، Bellingham will have to forget about his style of play last season، when he played as a free agent and will have to help his team's left-back in midfield this season. Real Madrid's coaching staff expects Camavinga to return to the pitch earlier than usual next week، paving the way for Ancelotti's plan to go ahead. Real Madrid are second in La Liga with 11 points from five games this season and four points behind leaders Barcelona

    Today is a historic day for the city of Duhok - iraq

    Today، the new season of the Iraqi Stars League will start with two matches of the Kurdistan clubs، and the league train will depart from Erbil and Duhok، and the president of La Liga will be in the stadium of Duhok club alongside Prime Minister Masrour Barzani and Adnan Darjal, the president of Iraqi football, will be present.The Emperor is back.

    Today Friday at the Franco Hariri Stadium in the first round of the Iraqi Stars League at 05:30 p.m.، Erbil Club will host the newly successful Diyala Club of the Iraqi Stars League، and before the match، Erbil FC coach Samir Baboi announced that like all other clubs، his club is not 100% ready for the tournament. For the first time in the history of the Stars League، Erbil enters the competition with a new coat of arms، bringing in 18 new players، in exchange for giving up 11 players and keeping six players from last season. In the first match of the Stars League, Iraq is scheduled to face the newly hosted club Diyala. Erbil FC has been preparing for the new season for about 40 days، playing four friendly matches during this period and playing against Naft، Duhok، Qasim and Amwakh clubs of Mosul، winning two matches and losing one match and drawing one in each.
